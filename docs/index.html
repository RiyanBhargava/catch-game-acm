üéØ How to Play

  1. Part 1: Move your mouse to control the basket
  2. Catch all falling items - don't miss any!
  3. Watch out for the person's face - it appears last and moves slowly
  4. Part 2: Click on tiles next to the empty space to move them
  5. Arrange tiles to form the complete ACM logo
  6. Complete both parts to reveal the secret message!
