ACM Catch Game

Test Your Reflexes & Catch Skills!

🎯 ACM Two-Game Challenge:

🎮 Game 1: Catch Challenge

Test your reflexes by catching falling food items with your basket!

  • 🍫 Chocolates: 2 points each (15 items)
  • 🍟 Chips: 3 points each (10 items)
  • 🍩 Donuts: 4 points each (8 items)
  • 🍕 Pizza: 5 points each (4 items)
  • 👤 Person's Face: 10 points (1 item - must catch to win!)

🎯 Objective: Catch the person's face to reveal the first part of the secret answer!

⚠️ Warning: If you miss 2 or more items, the game ends - be careful!

⏰ Time Limit: 2 minutes

🧩 Game 2: ACM Logo Puzzle

After completing Game 1, solve the slide puzzle to reconstruct the ACM logo!

  • 🔄 Click tiles adjacent to the empty space to move them
  • 🖼️ Rearrange pieces to form the complete ACM logo
  • 🧠 Use visual recognition - no numbers to guide you!

🎯 Objective: Complete the logo to reveal the second part of the answer!

🏆 Complete Challenge: Finish both games to discover the full secret message!
